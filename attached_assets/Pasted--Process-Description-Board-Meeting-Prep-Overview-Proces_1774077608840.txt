# Process Description: Board Meeting Prep

## Overview

**Process Name:** Board Meeting Prep

**Purpose:** Compile decision-ready materials for board and committee meetings.

---

## Process Description

The Board Meeting Prep process is a structured workflow designed to ensure that board members and committee participants receive comprehensive, well-organized, and timely materials to facilitate effective governance and decision-making.

### Key Activities

1. **Calendar Coordination**
   - Review board calendar to identify upcoming meetings
   - Confirm meeting dates, times, and participants
   - Align preparation timelines with distribution deadlines

2. **Information Gathering**
   - Collect management reports from relevant departments
   - Compile risk issues and updates from risk management
   - Review committee charters for agenda alignment
   - Gather supporting documentation and data

3. **Agenda Development**
   - Draft meeting agendas based on standing items and emerging topics
   - Prioritize discussion items requiring board decisions
   - Allocate appropriate time for each agenda item

4. **Board Pack Assembly**
   - Organize all materials into a cohesive board pack
   - Ensure consistency in formatting and presentation
   - Include executive summaries for complex items
   - Verify completeness and accuracy of all documents

5. **Action Tracking**
   - Update action tracker with items from previous meetings
   - Document status of outstanding actions
   - Flag overdue items for follow-up

6. **Distribution**
   - Distribute board packs within required timeframes
   - Confirm receipt by all board members
   - Handle any last-minute updates or corrections

---

## Inputs

| Input | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Board Calendar | Schedule of upcoming board and committee meetings |
| Management Reports | Operational, financial, and strategic updates from leadership |
| Risk Issues | Current risk assessments and emerging concerns |
| Committee Charters | Governing documents defining committee scope and responsibilities |

---

## Outputs

| Output | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Agenda Drafts | Proposed meeting agendas for review and approval |
| Board Packs | Complete meeting materials for distribution to members |
| Action Tracker | Log of decisions, action items, and accountability assignments |

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Measurement |
|-----|-------------|
| **Board Pack Preparation Time** | Duration from initiation to completion of board pack assembly |
| **On-Time Distribution Rate** | Percentage of board packs distributed by the required deadline |

---

## Success Criteria

- All materials are accurate, complete, and decision-ready
- Board packs delivered on schedule to all participants
- Clear documentation of actions and accountability
- Continuous improvement in preparation efficiency
